I love performing at colleges! It’s literally my favorite type of crowd because they’re young, fun and don’t file FIRs. I have a lot jokes about the education system, pop culture and social media that go well with this kind of a crowd. And we’ve created a standup, music, sketch and improv mixed show called Backbenchers which completely revolves around college life.
